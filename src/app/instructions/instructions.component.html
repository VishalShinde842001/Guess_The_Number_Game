Welcome to Guess The Number

Instructions

Welcome to Guess The Number! Here's how to play:

  1. Guess the correct number between 1 and 100.
  2. Enter your guess in the submission box provided.
  3. Receive instant feedback on whether your guess is correct or not.
  4. Your score will be displayed upon entering the correct guess.
  5. Your scores are stored securely in our database.
  6. You can freely navigate through all pages without the need to log in (except the 'Start Game' page).
  7. If you choose to quit the game, no data will be stored in the database.
  8. Explore the Leaderboard to see top performers.
  9. Check your best scores in the game.

Get ready for an exciting guessing experience. May the odds be ever in your favor!
